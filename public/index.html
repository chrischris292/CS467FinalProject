Introduction

We created an academia article data set by manually going into websites and downloading their papers. After, we applied sentiment analysis to the papers. By the end of our data collection, we created a data set that holds a vast amount of data for us to visualize down below.

Polarity Among Majors

We took data from our article data set and aggregated sentiment analysis scores of each paper. Our goal was to see which major has the most emotion in what is supposed to be an unbiased research paper.

As we can see above, psychology has the most polar sentiment among majors.
